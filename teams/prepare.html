Fire Protocol

If you encounter a fire on campus:

  • Activate the nearest fire alarm, and audibly warn other building occupants.
  • Leave the building and close all doors as you exit.
  • Call the Department of Public Safety (DPS) at (213) 740-4321. Give as much information as possible and stay on the line until the dispatcher ends the call.

If you hear a fire alarm:

  • When a fire alarm sounds, all occupants must exit the building immediately.
  • Even if you think it may be a false alarm, you must leave.

Earthquake Protocol

  • Drop: Drop to the floor, avoiding any falling objects or toppling furniture.
  • Cover: Take cover under a desk, table or other sturdy piece of furniture. If no such furniture is present, seek cover against an interior wall while protecting your head with your arms. Avoid windows, hanging objects, mirrors and tall furniture.
  • Hold On: If you take cover under furniture, hold on tight and be prepared to move with it. Do not release until the ground stops shaking.

Active Shooter Protocol

  • Run: Leave personal belongings; time is of the essence to get to safety.
  • Hide: Stay out of view; silence phones completely—including turning off vibration mode and hiding the lighted screen. When securing doors, use belts, cords, or other extenders to keep out of direct range of the door.
  • Fight: If you have no choice, fight the attacker using objects that can serve as weapons. If you are with others, work as a group to disarm the attacker until help arrives.

Places to Eat

Exploring Los Angeles is a big part of the 2026 Science Olympiad National Tournament experience, and that starts with food. From quick bites between events to sit-down dinners with your team, the neighborhoods around USC offer options for every taste and budget, all within a reasonable distance of campus.

Be sure to check each restaurant's website for current hours and large-group policies.

American

  • Hammy's Smash Burgers – Casual burger spot serving crispy smash burgers, fries, and shakes, great for a relaxed team meal or quick bite after events.
    Address: 2180 Westwood Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90025 — 10 miles from campus
  • Le Grand – French-inspired American brasserie in downtown LA with burgers, steaks, and pastas, better for a sit-down family or team dinner.
    Address: 707 S Grand Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90017 — 3 miles from campus
  • Fixins Soul Kitchen – Lively, family-friendly restaurant at LA Live serving fried chicken, waffles, and classic soul food sides that are easy to share.
    Address: 800 W Olympic Blvd Suite A150, Los Angeles, CA 90015 — 2 miles from campus

Italian

  • Bacari W Adams – Cozy small-plates restaurant near USC with many shareable veggie options, good for teams or families looking for a more relaxed sit-down meal.
    Address: 2308 S Union Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90007 — 1 mile from campus
  • Maccheroni Republic – Downtown pasta spot with house-made noodles, an inviting choice for a reasonably priced "nice night out."
    Address: 332 S Broadway, Los Angeles, CA 90013 — 4 miles from campus
  • Bestia – Trendy Arts District restaurant known for wood-fired dishes and creative pastas, ideal for coaches or teams celebrating a big achievement.
    Address: 2121 E 7th Pl, Los Angeles, CA 90021 — 5 miles from campus

French

  • Petit Trois – Classic French bistro known for dishes like omelets, steak frites, and pastries, great for families or small groups looking for a cozy sit-down meal.
    Address: 718 Highland Ave, Los Angeles, CA 90038 — 7 miles from campus
  • Perch – Rooftop restaurant with French-inspired dishes and sweeping city views, a memorable option for an evening in LA.
    Address: 448 S Hill St, Los Angeles, CA 90013 — 3 miles from campus
  • Cafe Fig – Hotel restaurant near LA Live serving American and French-leaning comfort food, convenient if you're exploring downtown attractions.
    Address: 939 S Figueroa St, Los Angeles, CA 90015 — 2 miles from campus

Mediterranean

  • Panini Kabob Grill – Casual Mediterranean restaurant with kebabs, wraps, salads, and rice plates that are easy to customize for kids and adults.
    Address: 600 W 9th St, Los Angeles, CA 90015 — 3 miles from campus
  • Bavel – Popular Arts District destination highlighting Middle Eastern and Mediterranean flavors, best reserved in advance for larger groups.
    Address: 500 Mateo St, Los Angeles, CA 90013 — 6 miles from campus

Hispanic (Mexican & Latin)

  • El Huero (Drive Thru) – Long-time USC favorite for quick tacos and burritos, especially convenient for late-night meals after long tournament days.
